About The Archive

The Journey of Sound: 1960 – 2030

This space maps out a long-form listening journey: curated albums and songs per year, tracing sonic evolution from 1960 to 2030.

Because full albums cannot be previewed cleanly in a web browser, each entry relies on iTunes API to pull a 30-second representative "Preview Track." Use the games to test your musical knowledge against the archive.
